# How to choose a Registration Platform

Before you onboard the Temu partner platform, according to your target sellers and the open API access you need, figure out how many apps you need to create and finish the onboarding following the SOP.

# 

Local Seller

For local sellers, registration is only required on the corresponding partner platform. For example, if you are developing an app for US-based sellers, you would register and apply as a developer at partner-us.temu.com. The same applies to European local sellers.

# 

Cross-border Seller

For cross-border sellers, Developers need to become developer on two Partner Platforms. This requires meeting two conditions:

1. 

You need to have a legal entity in China (including Hong Kong and other regions).

1. 

You need to have a company qualification in the United States or other regions.

You need to have a legal entity in the corresponding partner platform's region to legally register on the platform.

Since the registered Partner Platforms are different, developers of this type will have two different app_keys, and each app_key has different permissions. For details, please refer to the introduction of application types and permissions.
