# Registration Overview

Welcome everyone to register as a developer. We are very excited to have developers join our partner platform. Currently, [Temu](https://www.temu.com) has become one of the most popular marketplace platforms globally, offering millions of sellers the opportunity to sell their products. 

Before becoming a partner, you need to understand the following basic information to facilitate the subsequent cooperation. Let's get started!

# 

**Partner Type**

Before you begin, it's essential to understand the types of partnerships. We offer different partnership types for various business scenarios.

- 

**Developer: **Temu developers are development partners who provide system integration services for Temu.These developers typically offer e-commerce related systems, such as ERP systems, OMS systems or WMS systems, as well as MCM and Connectors.

- 

**Service Providers: **Service providers generally refer to service providers that offer services to sellers, such as operation agencies, product optimization service providers, and advertising service providers.

**Note that:** Currently, only Developer type is supported for online registration.We cooperate with Service Providers offline. If you are interested in cooperation, please feel free to contact us and send your letter of intent to [partner@temu.com](https://mailto:partner@temu.com).

# 

**Partner Platform Type**

As a Developer, Temu Partner Platform empowers you to streamline your daily operation, enabling you to provide seamless services to cross-border sellers and local sellers. It simplifies collaboration processes and offers valuable insights into your business.
