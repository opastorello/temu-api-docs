Due to the lengthy review time, please fill in your information accurately according to the following instructions before submitting it to ensure a quick approval of your company's qualification review.

Administrator Information

Administrator information should primarily be filled in with details of individuals closely associated with the company. Please note that this section is extremely important as it will determine the validity and authenticity of the partner's account.

- 

Roles within the business:

  - 

Beneficial owner (Any person who directly or indirectly owns 25% or more of the company's equity interests)

  - 

Legal Representative (The person legally responsible for business decisions and performance, including day-to-day management and compliance)

  - 

Both Beneficial owner and Legal Representative

- 

Legal Name

- 

Country of Citizenship

- 

Proof of Identity Type: Passport/Driver License

- 

Proof Document: Passport/Driver License document

**Important:** Please note that the Administrator **must** match the company information provided above. This means the Administrator must be the company's legal representative or senior management personnel. There must be publicly available documentation, ideally from a local government source, to prove the genuine relationship between the two. And it must correspond with the Roles Within The Business.

**Note:** The contact email address does not necessarily have to be the operator's real email address. You can fill in the email address used for technical support, contact personnel, or the company's external email address. This will not affect the review process.

Please triple-check all the information you have filled in before submitting to avoid delays caused by back-and-forth reviews.
