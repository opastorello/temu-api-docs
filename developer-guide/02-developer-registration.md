# Developer Registration

**Last update:** 2025-08-17 11:31:39

---

To quickly guide you to become a TEMU developer, here are some necessary steps you need to take:

# 
Sign up

- 
Sign up an account with your Email: [https://partner.temu.com](https://partner.temu.com)

**Note:** Developers in different regions have different registration websites, please pay attention to verification.

After your email is verified, click the `Create`  button to successfully create a partner account.

# 
Fill in Information

- 
Select Partner Type (If you want to create a Public app that serve Temu Seller): `Independent Software Vendor` 

**Note:** After confirming, the partner type can't be changed.

- 
Fill in the required onboarding information

- 
Business Registration Information

- 
Business Type: Sole Proprietorship/Corporation/Partnership

- 
Business Location: Company Registered Country

- 
Business Name

- 
Employer Identification Number（EIN）

- 
Business Termination Date

- 
Registered Business Address: Company Registered Address

- 
Registered Business Document: Company Registered document to show you are a legal company

- 
Administrator Information

- 
Roles within the business：

- 
Beneficial owner (Any person who directly or indirectly owns 25% or more of the company's equity interests)

- 
Legal representative (The person legally responsible for business decisions and performance, including day-to-day management and compliance)

- 
Both beneficial owner and legal representative

- 
Legal Name

- 
Country of Citizenship

- 
Proof of Identity Type: Passport/Driver License

- 
Proof Document: Passport/Driver License document

**Note: **The onboarding profile will be reviewed in 1 working day. Please contact product support team if it takes more than 1 working day.

# 
Create an App

- 
Please be sure to accurately fill in your successful application software development experience and provide relevant application website links, screenshots of the application's launch in other e-commerce platform service markets, proof of launch in other e-commerce platform application management modules, and explain clearly the MRD and PRD design documents of your software to help us better understand your development plan.

- 
Each entity can only apply to create one application for each application type

**Note: **The App Creation application should be reviewed in 1 working day, please contact product support team if it spend more than 1 working day.

# 
Implement and Test

- 
Create a technical support group, If you have not yet created a technical support group, please contact us via our technical support email: [partner@temu.com](https://mailto:partner@temu.com).

- 
Testing Cross Border Shop 

- 
Cross Border Seller Center：[https://seller.kuajingmaihuo.com/](https://seller.kuajingmaihuo.com/)

- 
if the verify code is needed, please contact product support team to get it

- 
Local Shop Testing Account:

- 
Local  Seller Center: [https://seller.temu.com/](https://seller.temu.com/), Please note that the seller backend address may vary depending on the region. Please double-check carefully.

- 
if the verify code is needed, please contact product support team to get it

- 
Testing APP information [See more](https://partner.temu.com/documentation?menu_code=0c59ed0f98c64f5aaec8f7641520eeec)

# 
Fill in Security Survey

- 
Fill in the Security Survey

- 
Sign Global DPA Documentation

- 
Submit survey with Signed Global DPA Documentation

# 
Publish App

- 
Apply for APP publishing

**Note:** Please make sure your app development has been done, and make sure the test url and test account can access your app and help Temu to verify your app function is ready to be published.

- 
The APP publishment application should be reviewed in 7 working day, please contact product support team if it takes more than 7 working day.

- 
Once your app published on service market：

- 
Temu Cross border seller can authorize your US app in: Temu Seller Central >  Service Market > Authorization Management

- 
Temu Local seller can authorize your App in: Temu Seller Center > Apps and services > App Store
