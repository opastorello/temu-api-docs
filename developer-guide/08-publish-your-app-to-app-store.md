# Publish your app to App Store

**Last update:** 2025-01-26 09:08:13

---

When your application development is complete and deployed to the production environment, you can begin preparing to release your application. After releasing the app, you will receive the official app_key for the production environment, and the app will be made public to all sellers.

# 
Prerequisites

- 
**Application Development:** Your application must be fully developed and functional.

- 
**Production Deployment:** Your application should be successfully deployed to the production environment.

- 
**App Store Preparation:** Ensure your app listing is ready for the app store, including descriptions, screenshots, and any necessary marketing materials.

# 
Publish App

Before officially releasing your app to the app store, you need to fill out an application form. Here's how to correctly fill out the application form:

- 
**App Target Seller:** This field specifies the target audience for your app.

**Important:**

- 
**Different Seller Types, Different App Stores:** Different types of sellers will have access to different app stores.

- 
**Adding, Not Removing:** Once you select a seller type, you can only add more, not remove any. This means you need to carefully consider your target audience. If you're unsure, please contact us for support.

- 
**Popular Platform:** Select the major marketplaces your app has already integrated with.

- 
**Product Testing:** Provide TEMU with test account information so we can verify the functionality of your app.

- 
**IP Address List：**

An IP request address refers to the requirement that requests initiated from the server must originate from a fixed IP address. Otherwise, the open gateway will reject these requests.

**Note: **Only non-Chinese cloud service providers are supported. IP addresses from countries such as China must not be used.

- 
**Redirect URL:** This is the callback URL, which is used if you choose to use callback authorization to obtain the seller's access token. If configured, it will take effect immediately. Please ensure that you have already developed this functionality.
