# How to obtain documents related to contacts and company relationships.

# 

For American company

The state government website can search for the contact person or provide official documents or endorsements (signed by the state secretary, stamped by the state government, etc.) that can prove the corresponding personnel's role

1. Statement of Information Corporation issued by the state government. The file should include fields such as company name, company address, and primary contact person.

2. Statement of Change to Add an Attachment issued by the state government. The file should include fields such as company name, company address, and primary contact person.

3. Articles of Organization with the state emblem or signature of the state secretary. The document should include fields such as company name, company address, and primary contact person.

4. Certificate of Formation with the state emblem or signature of the secretary of state. The file should include fields such as company name, company address, and primary contact person.

5. A business license (LICENSE CERTIFICATE) with the state emblem or signature of the state secretary. The file should include fields such as company name, company address, and primary contact person.

6. Annual Franchise Tax Report

If the above proof documents are not available, you can download the BOIR file.

The BOIR (Beneficial Ownership Information Report) document will contain information such as the subject name, primary contact person, registered address, etc.

For other companies

Other countries suggest filling in the contact person corresponding to Administrator information as the same as the contact person in the registration file, in order to ensure that TEMU can obtain the association relationship between related persons and companies.
