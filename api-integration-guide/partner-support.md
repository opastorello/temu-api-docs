# Partner Support

While we haven't finished building our ticketing system and online Q&A platform yet, we're still committed to providing technical support to help developers with integrations.

If you encounter any of the following difficulties, please contact us directly using the information below:

- 

**Product Requirements:** You can reach out to us with any questions or suggestions regarding product requirements.

- 

**Integration Questions:** If you have any questions or need clarification on technical documentation, or if you don't understand the meaning of certain fields, please don't hesitate to contact us.

- 

**Business Inquiries:** We are open to business collaborations. If you're interested in exploring deeper partnerships, please get in touch.

You can email us directly with any of the above issues, and we will arrange for someone to connect with you. Thank you for your support!

**Regarding our SLA (Service Level Agreement):**

We typically respond to emails within 24 hours, and most emails are addressed within 12 hours.
